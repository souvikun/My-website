Travelling fast and slow

"The world will be saved by beauty."
-Fyodor Dostoevsky

I had spent most of my life cycling, and part of it by walking. Even a small town like mine seem a lot bigger when you go slow. Perhaps, What I mean is that for humans value equals how unattainable something is. In fact distance is directly proportional to it's value, the further something is, the more valuable it becomes.

I say this because recently travelling with a motorbike has become very common for me, and somewhere in my mind my small town has only began to grow smaller and smaller. Imagine it as the opposite of the expanding universe, the world is shrinking.
